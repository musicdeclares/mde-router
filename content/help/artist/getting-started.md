## What is AMPLIFY?

AMPLIFY is a program by Music Declares Emergency that helps artists direct fans toward climate action. Your AMPLIFY link connects fans to vetted, grassroots climate organizations based on where they are.

**One link. Any country. Always works.**

---

## Your Dashboard

Your dashboard is your central hub for managing your AMPLIFY link. Here you can:

- View and copy your unique AMPLIFY link
- Generate QR codes for on-stage screens and printed materials
- Create and manage tours
- See how your link is doing

---

## Your AMPLIFY Link

Your unique link looks like this:

```
amplify.musicdeclares.net/a/your-handle
```

This single URL:
- Works anywhere in the world
- Automatically detects the fan's country
- Directs them to a relevant local climate org
- Never breaks, even between tours

### Where to Use It

- **Social bios**: Instagram, TikTok, Linktree, etc.
- **Stage visuals**: Display the QR code during shows
- **Merch & posters**: Print the QR code—it's permanent
- **Newsletters**: Include in fan communications
- **Video descriptions**: YouTube, TikTok, etc.

## Your Toolkit

Visit your toolkit page to get:

- **Your AMPLIFY link**: with copy button
- **Downloadable QR code**: print-ready, high resolution
- **Sample social captions**: ready to copy and customize

Find your toolkit at: **Dashboard → View Toolkit**

Share this page with your team so everyone has access to the assets.

---

## Account Settings

Visit the **Settings** page to:

- View your email address
- Reset your password
- Update your display name

Your handle (the unique part of your AMPLIFY URL) cannot be changed after account creation.

## About Music Declares Emergency

Music Declares Emergency is a group of artists, music industry professionals, and organizations who acknowledge the climate and ecological emergency and commit to taking action.

Learn more at [musicdeclares.net](https://www.musicdeclares.net/)
